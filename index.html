About company

This club aims to help Eastern Europe women to thrive their careers in the UK market.

Courses related to technical and soft skills, career development, seminars and networking events are the key elements provided. Along with a robust range of courses to build women’s confidence, which is pivotal for career ladder climbers.

We truly believe that everyone has something great to add value, therefore, women from all ages, careers and positions are more than welcome.

Grayscale includes full HTML, CSS, and custom JavaScript files along with SASS and LESS files for easy customization!
